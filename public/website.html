What is Autism Spectrum Disorder?

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental disorder that affects communication, behavior, and social interaction. Individuals with ASD may have difficulty with verbal and non-verbal communication, forming relationships, and adjusting to changes in routine. Early intervention and support can help individuals on the autism spectrum lead fulfilling lives.
